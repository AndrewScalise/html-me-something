Top Game Recommendations

  • Overwatch
  • Dota 2
  • League of Legends
  • Final Fantasy 14
  • Pokemon Omega Ruby/Alpha Sapphire

Overwatch

Overwatch is a first person shooter genre. This game has just been released this year. It has the hybrid makings of a fantasy first person shooter, where you are allowed to choose between a multitude of characters that have unique gameplay. The gameplay is solely online, and interactions are between teams of 6 vs 6. With such variations the game has a strategic flare that is both pleasant to play and watch as a competitve staple for FPS players.

Dota 2

Doat 2 is Massive multiplayer Online Battle Arena Game. In other words this game is a tower defense game. In this game the objective is to destroy your opponents base, and the first one to destroy the Ancient in your opponents base is the winner. This game is massive in strategy and variety of characters to choose from. It is also solely online and based on 5 vs 5 gameplay. Not to mention that this game is free to play, and only extra things like aesthetics can cost money.

League Of Legends

League of Legends is basically the counterpart to Dota 2. While the gameplay may seem simpler this game wins in popularity because again it is free to play, and has a large mainstream for not only playing, but also viewing as an E-sport.

Final Fantasy 14

Final Fantasy 14 is a massive online multiplayer role playing game. This game can be extremely time consuming, but is highly satifying with its story telling, and variety of gameplay in the fantasy world that is so beautifully made by Square Enix. It does cost money and is based on a montly subscription service.

Pokemon ORAS

Pokemon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire are remakes of the original Ruby and Sapphire games from the game advance. These games have been heavily renewed to the 3DS system, and feature many things such as more legendary pokemon, and an epilogue that is fantastic to play. Overall another great staple added to the Pokemon empire.
